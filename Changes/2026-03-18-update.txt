Date: 2026-03-18
Author: Claude (claude-sonnet-4-6)
Description: UI/UX updates and bug fixes across form sections 1–5, plus step reordering.

================================================================================
SECTION 1 – Personal Information
================================================================================

- Preferred Contact Method: Removed "Cell Phone" and "Home Phone" options.
  Now only "Email" and "Phone" are available.

- SSN / SIN Field: Removed automatic dash formatting entirely. Field now accepts
  raw digits only (max 9). Label dynamically switches between
  "Social Security Number (SSN)" and "Social Insurance Number (SIN)"
  based on the selected country.

- Current Address: Moved the Country dropdown to the top of the address block
  so that the State/Province and Zip/Postal Code labels update immediately
  based on region selection (USA vs Canada).

================================================================================
SECTION 2 – General Information
================================================================================

- Work Eligibility: Split the single "Are you legally eligible for employment
  in the United States or Canada?" question into two separate questions:
    1. "Are you legally eligible to work in the United States?"
    2. "Are you legally eligible to work in Canada?"
  Schema fields changed from legallyEligible to eligibleUSA + eligibleCanada.

================================================================================
SECTION 3 – Driving Experience
================================================================================

- Added "Auto Transport" as a new experience category.

- Replaced the year-of-experience dropdown with two number input fields
  per category (when driver selects Yes):
    - Years of Experience (integer)
    - Approximate Miles Driven (integer)

- Added an "Additional Information" text input below the number fields
  for each experience category.

- Removed the dropdown select for year selection entirely.

================================================================================
SECTION 4 – License Details
================================================================================

- Renamed "DOT Med Card Expiration" to "Medical Certificate Expiration".

- "Is this your current driver's license?" now defaults to false (unchecked)
  to avoid confusion when adding multiple licenses.

- "Is this a commercial driver license?" now defaults to false (unchecked)
  for the same reason.

================================================================================
SECTION 5 – Employment History
================================================================================

- Title changed from "Military & Employment History" to "Employment History".

- Info banner color changed from blue to amber/orange for better visibility.

- Updated banner text to include:
    - "Include any military experience."
    - "Please start from your most recent employer."

- Removed the standalone military service question from this section
  (drivers should enter military service as an employment record instead).

- Updated question label:
    "May we contact this employer at this time?"
    -> "May we contact this employer for employment verification?"

- Added two new Yes/No questions per employment record:
    1. "While employed here, were you subject to the Federal Motor Carrier
       Safety Regulations?"
    2. "Was the job designated as a safety-sensitive function in any
       Department of Transportation-regulated mode subject to alcohol and
       controlled substances testing as required by 49 CFR Part 40?"

  Schema fields added to EmploymentSchema: subjectToFMCSR, safetySensitiveFunction.

================================================================================
BUG FIXES
================================================================================

- Training & Education section was never rendered (hidden). Fixed by making
  it its own dedicated step (Step 6) in the form flow.

- Continue button on the Employment step was failing validation because the
  step was attempting to validate training/education/unemployment fields that
  were not visible to the user. Fixed by moving those fields to their own
  step with their own validation trigger.

- The final step (Certification & Signature) was rendering a blank screen
  because the STEPS array had a separate "signature" entry that pointed to
  no component. Fixed by merging Consent & Signature into a single final step
  backed by the existing DisclosuresAndSignature component.

================================================================================
STEP ORDER (New)
================================================================================

  Step 01 – Personal Information
  Step 02 – License Details
  Step 03 – General Information
  Step 04 – Driving Experience
  Step 05 – Employment History
  Step 06 – Training & Education
  Step 07 – FMCSR Compliance
  Step 08 – Document Upload
  Step 09 – Consent & Signature
